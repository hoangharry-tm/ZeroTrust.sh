# Risk Registry

All identified risks for the ZeroTrust.sh project are tracked here. This register covers risks relevant to the current analysis phase and the anticipated implementation phase. Risks are assessed before mitigations; residual risk post-mitigation is noted in the Mitigation column.

**Likelihood:** `Low` | `Medium` | `High`

**Impact:** `Low` | `Medium` | `High` | `Critical`

**Status:** `Open` — active risk | `Mitigated` — controls in place | `Accepted` — known and tolerated | `Closed` — no longer applicable
